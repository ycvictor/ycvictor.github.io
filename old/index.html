Yu Cheng Chen, from Tainan, Taiwan, plans to pursue a master's degree in computer science. He graduated from National Cheng Kung University (NCKU) in Tainan with a bachelor's degree with a double major in physics and computer science. He is passionate about the transformative power of machine learning to reduce costs and make innovative solutions more accessible. At NCKU, he conducted research on soccer player and ball trajectory analysis using computer vision and developed an AI system for the national soccer team.
