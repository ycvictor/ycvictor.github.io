## Sesame Cheesecake

This sesame cheesecake is rich, nutty, and intentionally low in sweetness.  
It uses only black sesame paste for flavor and has a dense, creamy texture with a classic cookie crust.

### Ingredients

#### Crust
<label><input type="checkbox"> Digestive biscuits 80 g</label><br>
<label><input type="checkbox"> Unsalted butter 40 g</label><br>

#### Sesame Cheesecake Filling
<label><input type="checkbox"> Cream cheese 350 g</label><br>
<label><input type="checkbox"> Sugar 40–50 g</label><br>
<label><input type="checkbox"> Large eggs 2</label><br>
<label><input type="checkbox"> Black sesame paste 90 g</label><br>
<label><input type="checkbox"> Heavy cream 140 g</label><br>
<label><input type="checkbox"> Cornstarch 10 g</label><br>
<label><input type="checkbox"> Vanilla extract ¼ tsp</label><br>
<label><input type="checkbox"> Salt a pinch</label><br>

### Procedure

#### 1. Make the Crust
1. Crush the digestive biscuits into fine crumbs.
2. Melt the unsalted butter and mix it evenly with the crumbs.
3. Pour the mixture into the bottom of the baking pan.
4. Press firmly using a spoon or the bottom of a cup to compact and level.
5. Refrigerate for 15–20 minutes to set.
    <figure>
    <img src="/images/recipes/Sesame Cheesecake-crust.png" alt="Sesame Cheesecake-crust" class="recipe-image">
    </figure>

#### 2. Make the Sesame Cheesecake Filling
1. Beat the cream cheese until smooth and lump-free.
2. Add sugar and mix until fully incorporated.
    <figure>
    <img src="/images/recipes/Sesame Cheesecake-cream cheese.png" alt="Sesame Cheesecake-cream cheese" class="recipe-image">
    </figure>
3. Add the first egg and mix well, then add the second egg and mix again.
4. Add black sesame paste and mix until the color is completely uniform.
    <figure>
    <img src="/images/recipes/Sesame Cheesecake-mix.png" alt="Sesame Cheesecake-mix" class="recipe-image">
    </figure>
5. Pour in heavy cream, then add vanilla extract and salt. Mix gently.
6. Sift in cornstarch and mix until smooth with no visible lumps.

#### 3. Bake
1. Preheat oven to 160°C / 320°F.
2. Pour the filling over the chilled crust.
    <figure>
    <img src="/images/recipes/Sesame Cheesecake-pour.png" alt="Sesame Cheesecake-pour" class="recipe-image">
    </figure>
3. Tap the pan lightly on the counter to release air bubbles.
4. Bake using a water bath for 60 minutes.
5. The center should jiggle slightly when done.

        <figure>
        <img src="/images/recipes/Sesame Cheesecake-finish.png" alt="Sesame Cheesecake-finish" class="recipe-image">
        </figure>
        <figure>
            <img src="/images/recipes/Sesame Cheesecake.jpeg" alt="Sesame Cheesecake.jpeg" class="recipe-image">

    </div>


#### 5. Cool and Chill
1. Let the cheesecake cool completely at room temperature.
2. Refrigerate for at least 6 hours, preferably overnight, before slicing.
